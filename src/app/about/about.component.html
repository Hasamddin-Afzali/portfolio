Who am i ?

"Unleashing Creativity, One Line of Code at a Time"

Hello there, I'm Hasamuddin Afzali, a passionate Full Stack Web Developer and Computer Engineer currently based in Karabuk, Turkey. Originally hailing from Afghanistan, I was born in 2000, and since then, my journey has been a pursuit of knowledge, technology, and creativity.

My academic journey began at Karabuk University, where I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Programming. However, my thirst for knowledge and my fascination with the world of computers and programming led me to further specialize in Computer Engineering. I believe that continuous learning is the key to staying relevant in the fast-paced tech industry.

As an ambitious individual with an eye for design, I am delighted to be a part of Birlik Ajans, an esteemed agency in Karabuk, Turkey, where I contribute as a skilled designer. I find immense joy in transforming ideas into visually captivating realities, and my work at the agency allows me to channel my creativity while solving real-world design challenges.

Web development has become the centerpiece of my career. Being a Full Stack Web Developer allows me to bring my ideas to life from both front-end and back-end perspectives. I thrive in crafting intuitive and user-friendly websites and applications that leave a lasting impact on users. My dedication to excellence drives me to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the field, ensuring that my projects are always cutting-edge.

With a solid foundation in programming and a knack for design, I take pride in creating seamless and visually stunning user experiences. My expertise encompasses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and other modern technologies that power the web. From conceptualization to deployment, I enjoy being involved in every stage of the development process.

When I'm not immersed in code or design, I love to explore the rich culture and heritage of both Turkey and Afghanistan. Embracing diverse perspectives has shaped my worldview and my approach to problem-solving.

This website serves as a portfolio of my work, showcasing some of my most exciting projects and demonstrating the progress I've made throughout my journey. Whether you're a fellow developer, potential collaborator, or someone looking to bring your digital ideas to life, I welcome you to explore my portfolio and get in touch.

Thank you for visiting my corner of the internet, and I hope we can connect and create something extraordinary together. Let's build a better digital world, one line of code at a time!
